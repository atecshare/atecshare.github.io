ATCM 3305 - Computer Animation I

ATCM 3305 - Computer Animation I (3 semester credit hours) Provides foundational knowledge of visual structure and problem solving in two- and three-dimensional design. Students will be introduced to design methodology and design processes with emphasis on the formal principles of composition and organization.
